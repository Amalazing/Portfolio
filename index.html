Hi, I am Lawrence Mueller

Web Developer

What I do

Full Stack

I enjoy working both the front and backend of the application. Working out bugs on the front end whether it be with styling, asynchronous code, or state managment is fun for me; plus I love seeing the result visually. While making the functionality of the application actually work by validating data, setting up routes, or managing a database is always interesting to me and I find a great deal of satisfaction when it all works properly.

Tools I Use

Personally I am partial to using the MERN stack. That means MongoDB, Node with Express, and React. These are simply my go to tools of choice when creating a web application. I find them simple and effective. For hosting I use both Heroku and AWS. I feel that AWS is a simpler option because it offers not only a DNS service via Route 53 but also acts as a Domain Registrar. This means you can buy and route your Hostname all in one place.

Learning

Since I am young, I know that I have a long way to go as a developer. I love learning and am always excited to pick up a new technology and develop with it. So besides just designing and making web applications in the stack I am comfortable with, I am always open to learning new skills to make me an even better developer.
